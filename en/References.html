Swiss Healthcare Card

Solar Energy Management System

Development of a controller that manges the flow of electricity between the power grid, local producers (photovoltaics) an energy store (vehicle-sized batteries) and local consumers. A first system of this kind has been deployed to the headquarters of the Brasilian army in Brasilia. The system has substantially reduced their electricity bill and assures power during grid blackouts.

  • Power Electronics
