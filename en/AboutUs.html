Founder

M.Sc. ETH. Ivo Graber is the founder and mastermind of Graber Software. He started programming when he was 12 and has not been seen far from a keyboard ever since. He has studied at the Department of Microengineering at Swiss Federal Institute of Technology Lausanne (EPFL) and Robotics at the Queensland University of Technology (QUT). Mr. Graber works since more than 20 years in Engineering and IT. In the process he has travelled all over the world working on complex engineering projects with people of diverse cultures. He is fluent in English, German, French and Italian.
